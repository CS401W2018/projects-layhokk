What are different ways to style your form?

There are many style my form such as inputs, buttons, and labels individually to make the form more readable and I use simple CSS properties to set padding, margin, border-radius, and colors for a clean look

Why does the professor like to use flex?

Because flex makes it easy to create responsive layouts without needing complex CSS or media queries. Flex items can automatically adjust their size and wrap based on the available space, which is perfect for forms that need to adapt to different screen sizes
