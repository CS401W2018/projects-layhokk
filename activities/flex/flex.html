Why do you need to have an accessible website? What does that mean?

Accessible websites generally provide a better user experience for all visitors, not just those with disabilities. Features like keyboard navigation, clear headings, and descriptive alt text can improve usability for everyone, including those using mobile devices or slow internet connections.
