What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is less suited for images requiring transparency, sharp edges, or text, like logos or illustrations. In such cases, formats like PNG are better suited.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIF is less suitable for high-quality photos, images with gradients, or complex color schemes, where formats like JPEG or PNG would be better choices.

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG is often preferred when image quality and transparency are critical, but it tends to have larger file sizes compared to JPEG.
