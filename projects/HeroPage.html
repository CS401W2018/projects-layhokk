My Hero-Aok Sokunhanha

Aok Sokunkanha is a popular Cambodian singer and a star in the music industry. She is a judge on The Voice Cambodia and Cambodia Idol. She is my favorite singer because she performs pop songs, and everyone supports her. I can say she is a great example of a Cambodian singer who supports the community and serves the people. Many of her songs are extremely famous nowadays.
