Camp Adventures

Camp Adventures is a program designed for adults seeking to experience thrilling adventures on the island. Participants from diverse backgrounds and cultures across the globe come together to engage in this enriching experience. The program is organized by Media Daily and offers a variety of activities to enhance participants' enjoyment and connection with nature. Highlights of the program include music, food, and dancing events held in the evenings, fostering a vibrant and communal atmosphere. We warmly invite all adults interested in camping to sign up and embark on this unforgettable journey. We look forward to welcoming you soon!

Tents

Each standard tent provided can accommodate two people comfortably. If you require a larger tent, please inform us in advance, and we will make the necessary arrangements.

Place to Bonfire

There are two designated locations for bonfires. To ensure safety, each fire will be closely monitored by a designated individual to prevent any potential fire hazards.

Place to Cook Food

All meals will be prepared by Media Daily. Participants are welcome to choose the foods they prefer. However, we kindly request that you avoid preparing excessive amounts of food to minimize waste. This space will be equipped with the necessary facilities to ensure a safe and enjoyable cooking experience.
